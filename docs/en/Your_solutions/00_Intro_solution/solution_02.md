## 2. Systems of Equations
**Equations:**
1.  $2x + 3y = 12$
2.  $x - y = 1$

**Solution (Substitution Method):**
* From Eq2, solve for $x$: $x = y + 1$
* Substitute $x$ into Eq1: $2(y + 1) + 3y = 12$
* $2 + 2y + 3y = 12 \Rightarrow 5y = 10 \Rightarrow \mathbf{y = 2}$
* Find $x$: $x = 2 + 1 \Rightarrow \mathbf{x = 3}$

**Result:** $(x=3, y=2)$
