## 4. Rearranging Formulas
**Goal:** Solve for gravity ($g$) in the pendulum period formula.

**Original Equation:** $T = 2\pi \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$

**Steps:**
1.  Divide by $2\pi$: $\frac{T}{2\pi} = \sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$
2.  Square both sides: $\left(\frac{T}{2\pi}\right)^2 = \frac{L}{g}$
3.  Isolate $g$: $g = \frac{L}{\left(\frac{T}{2\pi}\right)^2}$ or $g = \frac{4\pi^2 L}{T^2}$
