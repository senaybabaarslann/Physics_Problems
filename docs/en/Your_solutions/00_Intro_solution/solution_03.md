## 3. Proportionality (Law of Universal Gravitation)
**Formula:** $F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}$

**Changes Applied:**
* Both masses are halved: $(1/2 \times 1/2) = 1/4$ factor in the numerator.
* Distance ($r$) is doubled: $(2)^2 = 4$ factor in the denominator.

**New Force ($F'$):**
$$F' = \frac{1/4}{4} F = \frac{1}{16} F$$
**Conclusion:** The gravitational force becomes **1/16** of its original value.
