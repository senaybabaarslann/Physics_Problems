# Task 11 – Power & Energy

## Problem Statement

$R = 100\,\Omega$, $V = 50\,\text{V}$. Calculate power dissipated and energy consumed in 5 minutes.

## Theory

Power:

$$
P = \frac{V^2}{R}
$$

Energy:

$$
E = P \cdot t
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Calculate Power:**

$$
P = \frac{50^2}{100} = \frac{2500}{100} = 25\,\text{W}
$$

2. **Calculate Energy:**
   $t = 5 \times 60 = 300\,\text{s}$

$$
E = 25 \cdot 300 = 7500\,\text{J}
$$

## Final Result

- Power: $25\,\text{W}$
- Energy: $7.5\,\text{kJ}$

## Interpretation

Power represents the rate of energy conversion, while energy is the total quantity of work done over time.
