# Task 09 – Current

## Problem Statement

$Q(t) = 5t^2 + 5$. Calculate current at $t=3\,\text{s}$.

## Theory

Instantaneous current is the time derivative of charge:

$$
I(t) = \frac{dQ}{dt}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Differentiate $Q(t)$:**

$$
I(t) = \frac{d}{dt}(5t^2 + 5) = 10t
$$

2. **Evaluate at $t=3$:**

$$
I(3) = 10 \cdot 3 = 30\,\text{A}
$$

## Final Result

The current is $30\,\text{A}$.

## Interpretation

Since the charge function is quadratic, the current increases linearly with time.
