# Task 10 – Average Current

## Problem Statement

$Q = 30\,\text{C}$, $\Delta t = 2\,\text{ms}$. Calculate average current.

## Theory

Average current is defined as:

$$
I_{avg} = \frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

1. **Convert units:**
   $2\,\text{ms} = 0.002\,\text{s}$

2. **Calculate:**

$$
I_{avg} = \frac{30}{0.002} = 15000\,\text{A}
$$

## Final Result

The average current is $15\,\text{kA}$.

## Interpretation

Lightning bolts involve massive charge transfers over extremely short intervals, resulting in very high current magnitudes.
